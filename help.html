Sparx Reader Extractor - Help

How to Use the Tool

This tool allows you to extract text and questions from a Sparx Reader webpage. Follow the steps below:

  • Click on "Extract Reading Text" to extract the story text from the webpage.
  • Click on "Extract Questions & Answers" to extract any questions and answers presented on the page.
  • The extracted content will automatically be copied to your clipboard. You can paste it into an AI chatbot for further use.

FAQs

Q: Why isn't the text being extracted?

A: Ensure that the webpage contains readable text. If the page loads content dynamically, try refreshing the page and running the tool again.

Q: Why aren't questions being extracted?

A: Make sure the webpage contains identifiable question containers. If you're still having issues, feel free to contact us.
